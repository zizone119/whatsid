What is blockchain?

 Blockchain is a ledger management technology based on distributed computing technology that stores small data called 'blocks' in a chain-like connection-based distributed data storage environment based on the P2P method, so that no one can arbitrarily modify them and anyone can view the results of the changes . It is fundamentally a form of distributed data storage technology, and is designed to prevent arbitrary manipulation by the operator of a distributed node as a change list that continuously records changing data on all participating nodes . Blockchain technology is used for most cryptocurrency transactions, including Bitcoin . Since the cryptocurrency transaction process is written in a decentralized electronic ledger, servers are operated on each computer of many users running the blockchain software , allowing free transactions between individuals without a central bank.
